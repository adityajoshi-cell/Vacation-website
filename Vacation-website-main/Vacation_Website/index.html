History of the Park

Ranthambore National park is one of the most popular National Parks in India and even asia. The cPark was orriginally owned by a group of kings called Rajputs from the Kingdom of amber. Originally, the park was fortified with multiple forts to protect against Mughal invasions after the conquest, the kings of the area, and even the Mughals and British to hunt animals sich as the tigers and lepords that lived in the area. In 1955 after the indipendece of India, the area was named the Sawai Madhopur game Sanctuary.
